This text is about coating of ethernet cables.

Plenum (CMP) cables
Used in areas that have high air flow, like ventilations etc. Used in drop ceilings or under floors.
Have highest safety standarts, because they are fire resistant. In case they burn, the will not release toxins.


Riser (CMR) cables
Used on or in walls between floors in a bulding. 
Have lower safety standarts than CMP cables.


CM cables
Have the lowest safety standarts.
Used in regular rooms/officies.
